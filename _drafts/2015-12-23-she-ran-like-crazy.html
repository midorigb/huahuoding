原文：

She ran like crazy , praying none of them would catch up with her, as thousands of eyes were watching all around her.

She only stopped when she reached the finish-line as the winner of the marathon.

译文：

她发疯一般地狂奔，心里祈祷可以甩掉追赶她的那些人，而四周有成千上万只眼睛盯着她。

直到越过那条终点线，她才停了下来，这场马拉松的冠军诞生了。
