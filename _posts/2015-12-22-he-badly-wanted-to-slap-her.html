原文：

His palms itched, he badly wanted to slap her, he had to do it now.

His hesitation saved the mosquito’s life, again.

译文：

他又手痒了，真想扇她一巴掌，就现在，马上！

他的犹豫再一次让蚊子死里逃生了。
