两行故事
The sight of all the blood and mess
The sight of all the blood and mess made him extremely happy and he laughed like a maniac.

He smiled at his wife and lifted his first-born after 15 childless years.

混乱之中血迹斑斑，让他异常兴奋，笑得像个疯子。


他满脸笑意看着妻子，15年了，他们终于有了第一个孩子。
